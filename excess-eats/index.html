About Us


ExcessEats helps you order surplus foods from wherever you are. How it works: you type in your address, we tell you the restaurants that currently have excess foods for discounted prices near you. Want to be more specific? Search by cuisine, restaurant name, or menu item. We'll filter your results accordingly. When you find what you're looking for, you can place an order online and reserve a time slot for pick up. When your time arrives, just pop into the restaurant and pick up your food!
